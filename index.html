About

Leveraging strong communication and teamwork skills honed in healthcare, I transitioned to IT in 2021 as a QA Engineer at Brightest.

My focus on continuous improvement drives me to actively gather stakeholder feedback for software enhancements.

I collaborate effectively with developers to troubleshoot bugs, implement solutions, and streamline testing workflows through process optimization and automation.

A proponent of open-source solutions and efficient practices, I identify cost-effective alternatives to expensive tools and actively seek opportunities to expand my technical expertise in unit, API, and other relevant testing areas.

Sharing knowledge is important to me, so recently, I developed a training course on the Playwright testing tool, offered by Brightest and available in Python and Javascript.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I'm passionate about technical and non-technical advancements. I dedicate personal time to exploring areas such as embedded coding, as well as diving deep into the world of data, particularly data modeling and process mining. This ongoing exploration fuels my passion for innovation and informs my professional approach.
