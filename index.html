My Approach to Quality

I aim to support product excellence through a proactive, continuous, and analytical approach.

Moving beyond the traditional testing pyramid and following my own methodology, I strive to enhance/build more quality through what I term the 'Data-Driven Quality Loop (DDQL)' - a loop-like way of working that aligns testing efforts with real user behavior, insightful data, and evolving development needs.

My focus is not just on automation — though I implement robust UI and API testing — but on creating a feedback-rich loop where user insights, data-driven prioritization, and early collaboration help shape better decisions.

I embed quality thinking early (Shift-Left), support developers, and adapt testing based on production realities.
