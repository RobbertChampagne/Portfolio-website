Soft Skills

Before I started in IT, I worked in healthcare for a number of years.
This is where I mainly built up my soft skills: communication, teamwork, etc.
But my passion for IT took over.
After some training, I was able to start working as a QA engineer at Brightest in 2021.

On a project, I am constantly looking for improvement among various stakeholders.
What do they think could be improved? What bothers them? Are there bugs in the program they use?
I will look for these points, discuss them with the development team and help find a solution.
Optimizing and, if possible, automating the testing process is a priority for me.

I am a big fan of open source applications.
When I come across tools with a high subscription cost that do not fully meet my needs, I always try to find an alternative and preferably make one myself.
I also won't say no to unit testing. The more technical the better.

I like to share my knowledge, so I recently developed a training course on the Playwright testing tool.
This will soon be offered by Brightest (Details coming soon).

Recently I also started my childhood dream: electronics, Embedded coding and AI. More about this later.
